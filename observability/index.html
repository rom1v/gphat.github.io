My Background

In my early career I wrote some custom observability systems. Later I focused more specifically on charting libraries. At Twitter I worked on and managed the Observability team, which ran an in-house built system. Later, at Keen IO I inherited and extended the use of Datadog to a holistic observability platform I was very proud of. Most recently at Stripe I'm working each day to improve our observability! I've contributed to numerous projects in the space and continue to play with side project like client side charting libraries.
